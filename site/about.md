# About Lore Forge

## What Is Lore Forge?

Lore Forge is a **Story Bible Engine** — an AI-assisted narrative design framework that helps creators build and maintain living, breathing story worlds.

It treats story creation as **knowledge engineering**: worldbuilding, character psychology, timeline logic, memory structures, and narrative planning are organized into a durable, inspectable system called a **Story Bible**.

## What Is a Story Bible?

A Story Bible is the single source of truth for an entire story universe. It contains:

| Module | What You'll Find |
|--------|------------------|
| **Story** | Vision, themes, emotional core, timeline, chapter outlines, ending designs |
| **Characters** | Psychological profiles, core wounds, desires, fears, voice signatures, growth arcs |
| **Locations** | Sensory descriptions, atmosphere, function, secrets, chapter appearances |
| **Knowledge** | World rules, glossary of terms, canon facts, symbolism, emotional maps |
| **Memories** | Character memory graphs — what each character remembers (and what they misremember) |
| **Dialogue** | Annotated dialogue with emotional states, subtext, and relationship tension |
| **Quests / Events** | Quest dependencies, event chains, choice-and-consequence design |

Everything you read on this site is derived from an approved Story Bible. Nothing is invented on the fly — each detail has a traceable source in the knowledge layer.

## How to Use This Site

### Browse Projects

Go to the [Projects](/projects) page to see all available story worlds. Each card shows the project name and a summary of its contents.

### Read a Project

Click any project to explore its full Story Bible:

1. Start with the **Overview** (project summary)
2. Explore the **Story** section for themes, vision, and timeline
3. Meet the **Characters** — read their psychological profiles and arcs
4. Browse **Locations** for atmosphere and world-building details
5. Dive into **Knowledge** — the glossary, world rules, and symbolism
6. Read **Export** content — novel chapters and screenplay drafts

### Read Exported Novels

Many projects include exported novel prose under the **Novel** section. These are chapter-by-chapter drafts generated from the Story Bible, offering a traditional reading experience.

## The Philosophy

Lore Forge was built on a simple idea: **great stories deserve durable foundations.**

Traditional AI story generation treats narrative as a one-shot output: prompt in, story out. But real stories — especially long-running games, visual novels, and interactive fiction — need more than a first draft. They need a system that can:

- **Maintain consistency** across hundreds of scenes
- **Track character growth** without voice drift
- **Keep world rules solid** without contradiction
- **Support iteration** — writers should be able to revise without breaking canon

The Story Bible approach makes narrative knowledge durable, inspectable, reusable, and safe to update. Whether you're reading a single novel chapter or exploring an entire RPG world, everything traces back to the source of truth.

## Supported Genres

Lore Forge is designed for:

- **Narrative RPG** — Story-driven games with quests, choices, and consequences
- **Visual Novel** — Route-based narratives with character branches and multiple endings
- **Interactive Fiction** — Choice-tree and parser-based interactive stories
- **Tabletop Campaigns** — D&D and other TTRPG campaign planning
